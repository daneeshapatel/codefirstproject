What is Fashion Week?

Fashion week is a fashion industry event, which lasts approximately one week. It is where fashion designers, brands, or fashion houses display their latest designs in a runway show. Fashion Week occurs twice a year (A/W and S/S collections) in major fashion capitals.

What happens at a Fashion Week?

At Fashion Week designers showcase their clothing on multiple models who walk down a catwalk. The audience is typically seated during Fashion Week and are usually requird to dress according to a dress code. The audience can be offered transporation to the show, beverages and sometimes dinner after the show.
